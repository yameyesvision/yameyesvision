## OUTREACH PRINCIPLES
*(Read these before you send anything)*

**1. One message. One follow-up. That's it.**
You're not a salesperson. You're an author. If it's not the right time for them, respect that.

**2. Always read the profile before you write.**
A copy-paste DM is worse than no DM. If you can't reference something real about them, wait until you can.

**3. Lead with what you noticed about them — not what you need from them.**
The DMs that work start with the other person. Not the book.

**4. Never track who buys and who doesn't.**
Once you send it — let it go. Real giving doesn't come with a receipt.

**5. The goal isn't a sale. It's a connection.**
A sale is a transaction. A connection is a reader for life — and a person who tells two more people.
