## TIER 2 — Father's Day Play (June 15 target)

### 🔲 Set up Signed Copy Store (target: live by May 1)
Sell direct from your site. More profit per book than Amazon.

**The Bundle — "The Father's Day Gift Edition" — $25**
- Signed paperback
- Custom sticker
- Handwritten note
- *Rise of the Flames* early access / discount code (locks in next book buyers now)

**How to sell it:**
- Use **Gumroad** (free to start, small % fee) — easiest for a one-person operation
- List it as a physical product, note 5-7 day shipping
- Add a "Gift a Signed Copy" section to the website linking to Gumroad
- Position it as: *"Can't find a gift for Dad? Give him something real."*

**Why this works:**
- Print cost ~$8-9, you sell at $25 = ~$15-16 profit per book vs ~$3-4 on Amazon
- Sticker + handwritten note costs cents, adds perceived value
- Father's Day = natural gifting moment
- Ties next book in early = builds your list of buyers for Rise of the Flames

## TIER 3 — SEO Attack (SEMrush + Screaming Frog)

### 🔲 SEMrush — Do After GSC Is Set Up
Use SEMrush for:
- Find 5-10 low-competition keywords (e.g. "books for dads from sons", "father son memoir", "gifts for dad who reads")
- See what competitors rank for
- NOT for technical audits on a single-page site — that's overkill right now

### 🔲 Screaming Frog — NOT YET
Screaming Frog is designed for large sites with hundreds of pages. Your site is one page. Skip it until you have a blog or multiple pages. It won't help you right now.

### 🔲 Add a Blog / Content Page (Later — After Father's Day)
One article ranking on Google can drive traffic for years. Target something like:
- "What to write in a book for your son"
- "Father son books that will make you cry"
- "Gifts for fathers day that aren't junk"
