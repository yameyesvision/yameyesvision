Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

What We Collect

When you sign up for the YamEyes Vision email list, we collect your email address and, if provided, your name. When you submit a contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We do not collect payment information directly — purchases through Amazon or Fiverr are handled by those platforms under their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used to send you updates about YamEyes Vision — new content, book news, and announcements. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Third-Party Services

MailerLite is used to manage our email list. By subscribing, you agree to MailerLite's privacy policy. Formspree is used to process contact form submissions. Amazon and Fiverr links on this site are external — any transactions or data collection on those platforms are governed by their respective policies. We are not affiliated with or responsible for their data practices.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies required for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Third-party services embedded on this site (YouTube, MailerLite) may set their own cookies subject to their privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from our email list at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email. To request deletion of your data or for any privacy-related questions, contact us through the Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of this site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
