Author Bios

Short Bio (50 words)

Adam Llamas is the author of To the Son Who Saved Me: Ten Truths for the Road Ahead, a debut memoir about fatherhood, healing, and love. He is the founder of YamEyes Vision and a music producer based in the United States.

Long Bio (150 words)

Adam Llamas is a debut author, music producer, and the founder of YamEyes Vision, a creative platform built around real life, real experience, and real growth. His debut memoir, To the Son Who Saved Me: Ten Truths for the Road Ahead, is a deeply personal letter from a father to his son, sharing ten truths earned through lived experience about gratitude, honesty, accountability, and the transformative power of love. Before writing, Adam spent years working in music, using sound as a way to process life before he had the words. Fatherhood changed everything. It forced him to go inward, face what he'd been avoiding, and become intentional about what he was leaving behind. The result was a book and a creative mission built on a single conviction: that the most important thing you can pass down is the truth.
